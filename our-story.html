OUR STORY

Based in Shanghai American School, Eyeducate raises awareness about the prevalent issue of myopia and hyperopia (nearsigntness/farsightedness) which affects approximately 3.5 billion people today. As the use of technology is becoming ubiquitous in our lives, it is truly important for younger students to be educated about proper methods to protect their eyesight. Many younger children in more rural areas of China are not as educated about this topic, therefore, it is our goal to take this matter into our own hands.

Currently, EYEducate works directly wiht the Eye Love Vision Fund, a registered organization that has specifically designed a bilingual lesson to be taught to students around six years old, along with the support of Safe Kids China, a sub-branch of Safe Kids Worldwide and the Beijing United Charity Foundation to both teach students about how to protect their eyesight and to donate eye protection lesson toolkits to schools in rural China.

EYEducate is a non-profit organization based in Shanghai American School

We raise awareness about Ophthalmic Diseases and Disorders
