BRIDGET LU

FOUNDER & PRESIDENT

你好! Bonjour! Hello! I’m Bridget Lu, the Founder and President of EYEducate. With the increasing prevalence of myopia and hyperopia worldwide, particularly in China, I could no longer remain a bystander. In fourth grade, I became a part of this growing epidemic, and since then, many members of my community have also experienced these detriments firsthand. This is, after all, the dawn of the technological age. A screen in one hand; a book in the other; a pair of glasses resting on the nasal bone. Today, this is a common sight. Henceforth, it became my mission to educate our new generation of students to avoid making the same mistakes as mine. To learn how to properly protect their vision. Their eyes. From envisioning an organization dedicated to this cause, to contacting charities, ophthalmologists, and school administrators, to organizing the logistics of eye-related lessons and events, I have not only been fortunate enough to work with a unique team of like-minded high-schoolers, but also watch EYEducate expand and grow. I will continue to push EYEducate to reach new heights, widen our impact, and increase our scale to raise awareness of this often overlooked problem. Outside of running this organization, you’ll find me brainstorming solutions to urgent global issues in Model UN, debating about controversial topics around the world, playing music to make someone smile, using National Honor Society as a platform to raise funds for cancer research and malnourished children, helping teachers learn Chinese, playing badminton on the field, talking about philosophical dilemmas with friends in cafés, or writing and reading for fun. I sincerely hope for you to join our mission!
