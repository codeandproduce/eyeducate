EYEducate @ SAS International Fair

October 25th, 2016

On September 24th and 25th, EYEducate was given the opportunity to hold a booth at the Shanghai World Music Festival to spread eye awareness. There were hundreds of people in the audience and many were willing to learn about our cause as well as the issue of myopia and hyperopia that affects millions today.



On the 25th, EYEducate was also given 15 minutes to go on the centre stage and members shared their experiences, as well as the important methods that people can use to protect their eyesight. It was certainly not a traditional way of teaching the EYEducate lesson, but the team managed to morph its content with songs and short clips to make the learning interesting for the music festival attendees.



This was definitely a worthwhile experience and probably even more beneficial than our normal classes because not only children, but adults were also gaining important knowledge about their eyes.

EYEducate is a non-profit organization based in Shanghai American School

We raise awareness about Ophthalmic Diseases and Disorders
