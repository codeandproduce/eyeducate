VICE PRESIDENT

Hi! my name is Rachel, I am a student at Shanghai American School. I enjoy reading and creating new things in my free time. My favorite subjects in school are currently history and English, but I believe that philanthropic spirit spans across all interest and hobbies. I joined EYEducate because I really do care about the next generation, and people often overlook the importance of having good eyesight. I am proud to say that I am in a club that cultivates good habits, and feel incredibly honoured to be chosen as the Vice President of the club. Over the course of these three years I have taught countless children about the importance of eyesight, yet every lesson that I give I still find new ways that I can improve and engage with the children. Some of my responsibility include assisting the President, Bridget Lu, with planning and managing events, filling out corresponding forms, and helping keep the club on track. I hope that my actions will set precedent for what it means to be an EYEducate VP, and that the next generation will continue the expand what we have accomplished thus far. Next year we are hoping to expand our reach even further by arranging more trips to local schools and contacting more students at Shanghai American School to join EYEducate. We are also planning to have guided tours of eye glass manufacturing plants for members of EYEducate to deepen their knowledge. Additionally, we want to raise money to buy sunglasses for local labourers who spend long hours exposed to harsh sunlight that is damaging to their eyesight. Finally, I just want to thank EYEducate and the organisation Eye Love, and I hope that more people will take an interest in the work that we do.
