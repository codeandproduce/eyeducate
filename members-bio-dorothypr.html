Dorothy Li

Public Relations Officer

Joined 2015

Hi I’m Dorothy! I’m currently a student at Shanghai American School and the Public Relations Officer of EYEducate. I enjoy painting, designing, travelling, and many other miscellaneous activities that will take me hours to list out. Of course, I’m also passionate about EYEducate. I joined EYEducate because the problem we address is a growing concern in our century. With the rise of digital technology and an emphasis on schooling, many young kids spend hours on their digital devices as well as reading paper material. Sadly, I have damaged my eyes at a young age and I have personally experienced how nearsightedness is troublesome in mental, physical, and financial ways. Therefore, I realised how important it is to educate our next generation on how to protect their eyes — and that’s exactly what we are doing. Along with many of our dedicated club members, I have attended all the teaching events that have took place from the founding of this club. In the past three years, I’ve taught in local schools and migrant schools, schools in Shanghai and schools in Wuhan, classes from kindergarten and classes from grade three, and classes with ten students and classes with two hundred students. I have gained so much experience from all of these events, and my fear of standing in front of a classroom has turned into my love for interacting with these bright children. Apart from teaching, I have also been to several promotional events where we approach comers at music festivals and other large events to spread awareness of our club, our events, and our goals. I hope that EYEducate will keep on growing and creating a positive impact on our next generation!


WHY I JOINED

EYEducate is a non-profit organization based in Shanghai American School

We raise awareness about Ophthalmic Diseases and Disorders
