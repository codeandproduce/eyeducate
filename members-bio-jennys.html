JENNY LEE

SECRETARY

Hi! My name is Jenny Lee and I am the Secretary of EYEducate. Just like any other teenager, I spend a lot of time watching YouTube and reading online fan-fictions, which isn’t necessarily healthy for my eyes. I joined this club during freshman year and have participated in many club activities since then. Myopia is becoming increasingly common amongst young children, and a lot of this is linked to our bad habits (e.g binge watching YouTube videos for hours or even studying extra hours when lights are out). After providing some of these simple lessons to young children in various parts of China, I believe that this club really does provide a great opportunity to start combating myopia before it is too late.

EYEducate is a non-profit organization based in Shanghai American School

We raise awareness about Ophthalmic Diseases and Disorders
