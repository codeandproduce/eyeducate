ANNIKA WANG

Treasurer

Hi! My name is Annika and I’m a student at SAS Puxi. Outside of EYEducate I’m a member of the tennis team and love helping others. I’ve been a member since the beginning of sophomore year and am currently one of the officers who organize the events and keep track of the logistics of our club. I initially joined EYEducate because of my friends, but gradually I became more and more involved. You can find me at almost every EYEducate event! Although my knowledge of Chinese is rather basic, which means that I’m not an active teacher, I still help with the teaching sessions by taking pictures and organizing. I’ve also run the booth at International Fair, where we host a game in order to fundraise, and participated in promotional events hosted by the local organization we work with. Because I don’t have the best eyesight, I know personally that eyecare can be inconvenient and really expensive (glasses, contacts, etc) and not everyone can afford it. I really love what our club does because we’re making a difference in seemingly small ways. Having proper eyesight is essential for everyday life and by teaching children about proper self-care we can collectively help the prevention of worsening eyesight amongst the youth. One of my favorite moments in this club was when in my first year, I was able to run an EYEducate teaching event with another organization I work with outside of school. It was a lot of fun to get involved and see how happy and engaged the kids were. I hope that in my last year here I can help make even more of a difference!

EYEducate is a non-profit organization based in Shanghai American School

We raise awareness about Ophthalmic Diseases and Disorders
